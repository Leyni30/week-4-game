Crystal Collector

You will be given a random number at the beggining of the game. There are 4 chrystals bellow. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score. You win the game by matching your total score to random number. You loose the game by going above the random number. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it. Each time the game starts it will change the values behind the pictures.
